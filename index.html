About the Game

WhackAEllie is an exciting arcade game that combines classic whack-a-mole mechanics with modern roguelike elements. Test your reflexes, build powerful combos, and unlock upgrades as you progress through increasingly challenging rounds!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?
Tap on animals as they pop up to whack them! Build combos for higher scores and earn currency to purchase upgrades between rounds.
What are upgrades?
Between rounds, you can purchase various upgrades to improve your abilities, increase your score multipliers, and gain special powers to help you progress further.
How does the roguelike system work?
Each run is unique with randomly offered upgrades. If you fail, you start over with a fresh set of challenges and opportunities!
Is there sound in the game?
Yes! WhackAEllie features engaging sound effects. You can adjust or mute the audio in the game settings.
The game is running slowly. What can I do?
Try closing other apps running in the background. Make sure your device has sufficient free storage space.
